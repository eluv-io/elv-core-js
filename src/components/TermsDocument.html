  1. Service Commitment and Support.
  1. Service Levels.  With respect to both Content Serving and Content Management, Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Platform Services available with a Monthly Uptime Percentage of at least 99.9% during any monthly billing cycle.  "Monthly Uptime Percentage" shall be calculated by subtracting each of the Content Serving Error Rate and the Content Management Error Rate from 100%.  For purposes of this Section 4, (i) the "Content Serving Error Rate" is the percentage calculated by dividing (x) the number of minutes in a monthly billing cycle during which the Platform Services returned an internal error to an Authorized User attempting to access, stream, or download content from the Fabric, by (y) the total number of minutes in such monthly billing cycle; and (ii) the "Content Management Error Rate" is the percentage calculated by dividing (x) the number of minutes in a monthly billing cycle during which the Platform Services returned an internal error to an Authorized User attempting to manage content objects on the Fabric.  Notwithstanding the foregoing, the error minutes used to calculate the Content Serving Error Rate and the Content Management Error Rate shall not include any errors caused by forces beyond Provider’s reasonable control.
  2. Service Credits.  In the event the Monthly Uptime Percentage with respect to either Content Serving or Content Management (each, a "Service Segment"), each as calculated in accordance with Section 4(a), is less than 99.9%, Customer will be entitled to a credit calculated as a percentage of the Fees paid during such monthly invoice period specifically toward Content Serving and/or Content Management, whichever experienced the Monthly Uptime Percentage of less than 99.9%, as follows (a "Service Credit"): (i) if the Monthly Uptime Percentage is at least 99.0% but less than 99.9%, a Service Credit toward the applicable Service Segment of 10%; (ii) if the Monthly Uptime Percentage is at least 95.0% but less than 99.0%, a Service Credit toward the applicable Service Segment of 25%; and (iii) if the Monthly Uptime Percentage is less than 95.0%, a Service Credit toward the applicable Service Segment of 100%.  Service Credits shall be applied only to their applicable Service Segments and only against future Fees owed by Customer, and under no circumstances will entitle Customer to a refund or any other payment from Provider.  Service Credits are not transferrable.  To be eligible to receive a Service Credit, (x) Customer must send a request via email to Customer Support at support@eluv.io, (y) such request must be received by Provider by the end of the second billing cycle after the monthly billing cycle for which the Service Credit is claimed, and (z) the request must include the dates and times of the non-zero error rates claimed by Customer as the basis for the Service Credit request.
